### üë§ &nbsp; About me 

I am a developer with 10+ years of experience. My first years were dedicated to electronics and C language applications, and since 2018 working as a Python Backend Engineer. Along the way I have worked in different roles and technologies, had my own startup, worked as a teacher and have my own tech platform and obviously as a programmer. These combinations gave me a wide experience to approach the work from different points of view and applying a high quality to the day-to-day work.

### üíª &nbsp; My Website
     
Having my website was one of my most wanted wishes. When I started to working as a teacher I could find a place where I can write articles about topics I am interested in and also creating projects that make the exposed theory a reality, and that is useful for teaching as well as all of the people there. So, after more than two years delivering materials in different flavours I achieve the goal to get my own tech platform, **Goto IoT.** The aim of the platform is to have a website to publish articles about techonologies, a Github Organization to create projects extremelly well documented and easy to use and a community where the members can interact to. 

Due to I decided to focus my efforts in Goto IoT, I have been moving most of my repos there. The projects I have here are the ones that don't fit within the Goto IoT sphere (yet). A quick note about Goto IoT is that its target are Spanish speakers, so you probably have to translate the content if you don't speak Spanish.
